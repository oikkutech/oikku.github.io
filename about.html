About Us

We are an EdTech startup based out of San Diego designing interconnected, personalized communities by leveraging mobile mediums.


We've been tutoring and working with both kids and senior citizens in tech for the past 4 years. Our products are designed around those interactions to be as easy and accessible to use as possible, while still engaging and fun.


Our mission is to teach the world tech literacy, and it can start with you! Get in contact with us at pygeonapp@gmail.com. We'd love to talk.
